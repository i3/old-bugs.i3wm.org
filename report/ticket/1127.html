Description

I have focus-follows-mouse configured. At random intervals this bit gets lost and focus fails to follow mouse, requiring click to-focus.

This is particularly annoying when, as often happens, I mouse from an Emacs window to a terminal, type to the terminal, and step on text in my Emacs buffer instead. However, because Mod1-R restores focus-follows-mouse, this is probably not specifically related to anything Emacs is doing.

Observed on 4.6-45-gfd07f98, also on the stock 4.5 distributed with Ubuntu 13.10, I'm pretty sure the bug began to manifest when I upgraded from 13.04; if that shipped a different i3 version it may be a clue to when the regression took place.
