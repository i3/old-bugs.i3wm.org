Thanks for your patch. Here are a few comments on it:

  1. Please rename the configuration member from no_edge_border to hide_edge_border. I always feel slightly ill at ease when stumbling upon a double negative (no_edge_border disabled by default).
  2. Typo: "usefull" spells "useful"
  3. Please use parenthesis around mathematical expressions such as con->rect.x + con->rect.width (line 939 in src/con.c).
  4. Please don’t put a condition and a statement on the same line (line 898 of src/con.c)
  5. Instead of the rather cryptic "adj", please call the variable "adjacent_to" and rename ADJ_LEFT to ADJ_LEFT_SCREEN_EDGE (likewise for ADJ_RIGHT).
  6. Please call your type something ending in _t, for example adjacent_t.
  7. Afterwards, please provide one single patch (not multiple ones). Use git rebase -i to squash multiple commits into one.

Good job! Looking forward to the next revision of your patch.
