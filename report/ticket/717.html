I assume you are hitting the limitation of the data type of the width of a CreatePixmap request, which is UINT16 (so, 65535). A 65535-character long status line is in the worst case (every character being a 4-byte UTF-8 glyph, and having a really small font with, say, 6 px per character) 98302 pixels. I seriously doubt that you have that much screen space on a single output and thus consider your use-case theoretical.

I don’t want to "fix" pathological cases like that. Fix your input script so that it prints a reasonably long status bar.

In case your limit was much lower, please specify your actual script and not a constructed example like cat /dev/urandom.
