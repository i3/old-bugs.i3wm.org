comment:2 Changed 17 months ago by tony@â€¦

I cannot reproduce this by installing KDE, but I found a simpler model for the error.

To reproduce:

Open two terminals on a workspace and a third terminal on a different workspace.

On the third terminal, type $ sleep 5; exit. Switch to the first workspace and begin drag resizing. Continue resizing for approximately 5 seconds. When the third terminal receives the exit command, the drag will be cancelled.

Quite spurious. I'm working on the solution now.
