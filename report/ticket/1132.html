To clarify:

Is the logfile actually from a session where this hang happened? If so, at what time specifically did it hang?

When the hang occurs the next time, try SSH'ing into your machine (or maybe switching to a different VT might work, too) and run i3-msg -t get_version. If that still works, i3 does not hang, and something else is to blame.
