Triple monitor status bar mismatch

Description

I have i3 installed on arch on multiple computers with entirely different specs except that they both have three monitors at 1920x1080 each. In both cases, the status bar looks wrong, whether at the top of the screen or the bottom.

In one case, there is a status bar for each screen, but the status bar is associated with the wrong screen--if workspace two is assigned to the center screen, the i3 status bar for workspace two is on the right screen; workspace three is on the right screen, but the status bar is on the left screen; workspace one is on the left screen, but its status bar is on the center screen.

In the other case, the center screen is correct: the status bar is on the correct screen, and it correctly corresponds to workspace two which is on the center screen. However, instead of having the other two status bars on their correct screens, they're both on the left screen.

This may be related to the fact that I have to mix connectors to get three screens--in each case, one is powered by HDMI while the other two are DVI.
