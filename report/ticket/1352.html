comment:3 Changed 7 months ago by peterN

Big thanks for your quick response! My issue is fixed, though it was not related to whitespace but had to do with my setup/startup procedere.

  • lightdm start's both monitors with a clone configuration
  • in ~/.i3/config I had an exec command that changed screen setup via xrandr

Now I moved the script with the xrandr execution from ~/.i3/config to ~/.xsessionrc and this seems to do the trick.

I found that adapting the tray_output and restarting i3 also did work quite nice, which made me rethink the startup/intialize process.
